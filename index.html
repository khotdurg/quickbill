Sharing this app with your team

Share the app link with anyone on your team. When they open it on their phone and sign in with their own Google account, QuickBill creates a private copy of the database in their Drive — separate from yours.

To let a teammate work on the same customer database as you: open Google Drive on the web, find the QuickBill Data folder, right-click → Share → add their email with Editor access. They then use the "Use a shared database" option below.
